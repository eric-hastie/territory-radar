How the signal score works

Every account's score is a transparent, weighted sum of verified signals, so the ranking is explainable - no black box, no vibes. A relevant open role is worth ×10, recent funding +20, new leadership +20, and an expansion or new region +12. On top of that, verified people signals add a boost: a senior tech executive (CTO / CIO / relevant VP or chief) newly in seat within about 4 months +25, a QA-leadership req that just disappeared from the board (position filled - a buyer landing) +25, and a new director in seat +15. Territories that carry developer and tester estimates also add a coverage-gap boost from the estimated dev:tester ratio - +10 at 12:1 or wider, +5 at 8:1 - fixed thresholds rather than percentiles, so the cutoff stays stable and explainable as the territory grows. Both counts are labeled estimates (LinkedIn title sampling, req history, and industry norms), so the ratio feeds coarse buckets, never precise ranking.

Tiers: Hot at score ≥ 60, Warm at ≥ 40, Watch below that. The thresholds flex per territory because a "lot of hiring" means different things in different markets.

Methodology. Demo territory of 20 real companies; signals verified July 13, 2026 against live ATS job boards (Greenhouse / Lever / Ashby / Workday and others) and public sources, re-verified weekly.
