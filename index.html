Territory Radar

An automated territory-research tool for Account Executives. It scores every account in a sales territory on real buying signals - hiring, funding, leadership moves, and expansion - and tracks which accounts are heating up, so account planning starts from "why now," not a static list.

Built by Eric Hastie · demo territories · updated July 13, 2026 · a portfolio project

Illustrative demos. Each board is run as if I were an AE selling a named vendor's product (or an unnamed one) - none of these vendors is affiliated with or has endorsed this project. The companies, job postings, funding rounds, and leadership moves are real, verified July 13, 2026 against live ATS job boards (Greenhouse / Lever / Ashby) and public sources; the scoring and tiering are this tool's own. A live deployment runs against a real book of business in a private repo.

Every account's score is a transparent, weighted sum of verified signals: relevant open roles × 10, recent funding +20, new leadership +20, expansion +12. Each board explains its own tiers.

Still curious where this started? The original demo board (June-July 2026) - a cloud-infrastructure territory tracked across four weekly snapshots - is still live, with its momentum history and open-roles inventory.
