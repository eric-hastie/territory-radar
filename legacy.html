About this board

This is the original Territory Radar territory: 16 real mid-market and enterprise companies scored on buying signals for a cloud infrastructure / DevOps platform, tracked across weekly snapshots since June 2026. Job postings are one of the cleanest, earliest intent signals in B2B - a company scaling its platform team or hiring an infra leader is telling you about budget and initiatives before any intent-data vendor flags it. The site has since grown into four industry demo territories; this page is preserved because it carries the longest momentum history.

How the signal score works

Every account's score is a transparent, weighted sum of verified signals, so the ranking is explainable - no black box, no vibes. A relevant open role is worth ×10, recent funding +20, new leadership +20, and an expansion or new region +12.

Tiers: Hot at score ≥ 80, Warm at ≥ 40, Watch below that. The thresholds flex per territory because a "lot of hiring" means different things in different markets.

Methodology. Sample territory, signals verified against company career pages / ATS and public sources. Signal counts and firmographics are best-effort from public data. This is illustrative demo data on real companies - a live deployment runs against a real book of business in a private repo.
