About this project

This started as the engine behind a remote-AE job search - a pipeline that screens companies against a profile, verifies live job postings, and tracks change week over week. That job-hunt build was the prototype; Territory Radar re-points the same engine at the other side of the desk: instead of finding jobs, it finds buying signals across a sales territory. Job postings are one of the cleanest, earliest intent signals in B2B - a company scaling its platform team or hiring an infra leader is telling you about budget and initiatives before any intent-data vendor flags it. The site has since grown into three industry demo territories; this page preserves the original board.
